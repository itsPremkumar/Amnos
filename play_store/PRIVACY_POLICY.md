# Privacy Policy for Amnos Browser

**Last Updated**: April 11, 2026

Amnos ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage for the Amnos Android application.

## 1. Zero Data Collection Policy
Amnos is designed as a privacy-hardened, local-only browser. 
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, such as your name, email address, or phone number.
- **No Browsing History**: Your browsing history, cookies, and cache are stored strictly in volatile memory (RAM) or designated internal storage that is wiped at the end of every session. We do not have access to your browsing data.
- **No Analytics/Tracking**: Amnos does not include any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not track your behavior within the app or across the web.

## 2. Permissions
Amnos requests the following permissions for functional purposes only:
- **INTERNET**: Required to access websites and provide browsing functionality.
- **Network State**: Used to detect connectivity and manage the local proxy.

## 3. Security Features
Amnos implements several layers of security to protect your privacy:
- **Hardened WebView**: We enforce HTTPS-only connections and block known trackers and malicious scripts.
- **Fingerprint Mitigation**: We randomize and obfuscate browser fingerprinting entropy (e.g., UA strings, screen resolution) to prevent cross-site tracking.
- **WebRTC/WebSocket Blocking**: We disable protocols that are commonly used to leak real IP addresses or establish persistent connections.

## 4. Third-Party Websites
Amnos allows you to visit third-party websites. These websites have their own privacy policies. We do not control and are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of any third-party sites you visit.

## 5. Children's Privacy
Because Amnos does not collect any data, it is inherently safe regarding children's privacy. However, we encourage parents to monitor their children's internet usage.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App's documentation or repository.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the security of Amnos, please contact us via the project repository: https://github.com/itsPremkumar/Amnos
